PERSONAL STATEMENT

I am Zeyu Han, an undergraduate student in the College of Software Engineering at Sichuan University, China. With a deep interest in machine learning and deep learning, I am excited to apply for the Sunstella Machine Learning Research Summer Camp to gain more research experience for my upcoming CS PhD application in Fall 2024.

Throughout my undergraduate studies, I have consistently excelled in my coursework, achieving a cumulative GPA of 3.9 out of 4.0 and a perfect 4.0 GPA in all of my major courses. As a result, I am ranked in the top 2%. Through my coursework, I have gained a strong foundation in programming languages such as C, C++, Java, and Python. I am excited to apply these skills to my research pursuits as I embark on my summer camp journey.

In addition to regular courses, I pursued a research internship advised by Prof. Yan Wang working at School of Computer Science, Sichuan University in the autumn of 2022. During my internship, I had the opportunity to work on two research projects that focused on medical image analysis using machine learning techniques, providing me with valuable experiences and knowledge.

The first project focused on developing an assistive system for predicting radiotherapy dose distribution for cancer patients. This project was primarily based on Domain Adaptation (DA), which aims to transfer knowledge from a source domain with abundant labeled training data to a target domain with fewer data, a common challenge in medical image analysis. This project resulted in an accepted paper at the IEEE International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging (ISBI) 2023 conference and its journal version, which is currently under review at the International Journal of Intelligent Systems (IJIS).

Currently, I am working on my second research project, which aims to reconstruct low-quality PET images into high-quality PET images using diffusion models. I have designed the whole approach, analyzed experimental results, and drafted the manuscript. The paper has been submitted to the Medical Image Computing and Computer Assisted Intervention Society 2023 (MICCAI 2023) conference, and I am eagerly awaiting the results.

These research experiences have allowed me to develop critical thinking skills and hone my abilities in reading and writing academic articles. Additionally, working on these projects has taught me the importance of teamwork, time management, and effective communication skills in research. Despite the challenges, I feel a sense of joy and excitement when the experimental results demonstrate my assumptions and when my proposed method proves effective in real-world applications.

I believe attending the Sunstella Machine Learning Research Summer Camp will be a challenging and enjoyable journey for me. My discipline, diligence, and alacrity make me a qualified candidate for the application. Thank you for considering my application, and I look forward to hearing back from you.
